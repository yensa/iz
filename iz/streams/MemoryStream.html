public class MemoryStream : Stream, StreamPersist, FilePersist8

Implements a stream of contiguous, GC-free, heap-memory.

In theory its size can go up to 2^31 bytes (X86) or 2^63 bytes (X86_64). This value is obviously limited by the amount of DRAM and the fragmentation. MemoryStream is also enhanced by implementing the interfaces StreamPersist and FilePersist8. They allow to save the content either to another stream or to a file and to load the content either from another Stream or from a file.

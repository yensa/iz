public struct CharMap

CharMap is an helper struct that allows to test if a char is within a set of characters.

Example

CharMap cm = CharMap['a'..'f', '0'..'9' , 'A'..'F', '_', 9];
assert('a' in cm);
assert('b' in cm);
assert('c' in cm);
assert('d' in cm);
assert('e' in cm);
assert('f' in cm);
assert('g' !in cm);
assert('A' in cm);
assert('B' in cm);
assert('C' in cm);
assert('D' in cm);
assert('E' in cm);
assert('F' in cm);
assert('G' !in cm);
assert('0' in cm);
assert('4' in cm);
assert('9' in cm);
assert('_' in cm);
assert('%' !in cm);
assert('\t' in cm);
